Meditation Tips

1. Find the most comfortable position to help you relax. It can be when sitting, standing, lying down or walking.

2. Loose and comfortable clothes help because tight fitting clothes can make you feel tense.

3. The place where you meditate should have a soothing atmosphere.

4. Set a time limit and use all the time you have allowed.

Breathing Tips

1. Keep your attention on your inhale and exhale.

2. Take a deep inhale, expanding your belly, and then exhale slowly, elongating the out-breath as your belly contracts.

3. Breathe in for a count of 7, then breathe out for a count of 11. Continue for 5 - 10 minutes or longer if you can, and enjoy the calming effect.

4. Breathe in through your nose and out through your mouth.
